All about the Mets baby
When I hear the words "it's all about the Mets baby", I am filled with a deep feeling of dread. The Mets are not artifacts of tomfoolery. The dread comes not from a place of supreme fandom, but mearly from the knowledge that the life fire of the one who spake those words is dwindling.

The sweet chk-chk-chk of the rotating barrel of the Soviet T-34-85 medium tank alerted me to exactly how short of a life span the Mets tomfooler had remaining.

The 500 horsepower Model V-2-34 38.8 L V12 Diesel engine revved, nothing more than a battle cry. I heard the clunk of a APCR armor-pericing shell loading into the 76.2 mm F-34 mounted gun, they were going for show this time.

I shant detail the events as there is not much left to cover by the end. Just take this as a warning.

Speaking of WAR-ning, let's hear from today's sponser, War Thunder.
